⚠️ The Problem

📚

Document Overload

Thai law firms manage thousands of contracts, regulations, and policy documents. Finding specific clauses or cross-references takes hours of manual work.

⏱️

Slow & Expensive Reviews

Contract review, due diligence, and compliance checking are labor-intensive — junior lawyers spend 60-70% of time on document review.

🤖

AI Hallucination Risk

Standard AI chatbots fabricate answers. For legal work, a single hallucinated clause reference can lead to catastrophic outcomes.

🔍

No Traceability

Existing AI tools provide answers with no way to verify against source documents. Lawyers cannot trust what they cannot trace.

⚖️ In legal work, wrong answers are worse than no answers. AI must be verifiable.

💡 Our Solution — Evidoc

AI document intelligence that NEVER hallucinates.

🎯

Deterministic Answers

Every answer is extracted directly from your documents — not generated. Knowledge Graph technology ensures factual, traceable responses.

🔗

Click-to-Verify Citations

Every evidence sentence links back to the exact location in the original PDF. One click → highlighted text on the source page.

🧠

Knowledge Graph Intelligence

Automatically maps entities, relationships, and communities across all documents — finds connections humans miss.

🔒

Private & Secure

Your documents stay private. Enterprise-grade security with Azure AD, RBAC, and tenant-level data isolation.
