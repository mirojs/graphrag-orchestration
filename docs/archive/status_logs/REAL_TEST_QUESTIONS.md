# Real Test Questions (Based on 5 Phase 1 Documents)

## 1. BUILDERS LIMITED WARRANTY.pdf
*Context: A warranty document usually defines coverage periods, exclusions, and claim procedures.*
- **Vector (Quote):** "What is the exact duration of the structural warranty coverage?"
- **Local (Entity):** "Who is the 'Warrantor' listed in the agreement?"
- **RAPTOR (Theme):** "Summarize the exclusions related to plumbing and electrical systems."
- **DRIFT (Reasoning):** "How does the homeowner's maintenance responsibility affect the validity of a structural claim?"
